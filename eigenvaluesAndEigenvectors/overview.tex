The vector $\bf v$ is an eigenvector of $A$ with eigenvalue $\lambda$ if
\[
A{\bf v} = \lambda{\bf v}
\]
meaning multiplying $\bf v$ on the left by the matrix $A$ has the same effect as multiplying it by the scalar $\lambda$.
